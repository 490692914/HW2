Background

Hello! I’m Shan Xiao. I’m from the University of Iowa, a junior majoring in Management Information Systems (MIS) and Finance. I come from Changsha, a city in the south of China. When I was 16 years old, I left my home town and flew across the Pacific Ocean for studying here. I came to America because I want to experience the “American Dream”. The University of Iowa has a welcoming environment for international students. Now, I am addicted to live in this peaceful and passionate college town.
